Unmissable

macOS menu bar app

Never miss a meeting again

Unmissable lives in your menu bar and shows full-screen blocking overlays before meetings start, so you always join on time.

Calendar integration

Connects to Google Calendar and Apple Calendar. Reads your events and detects meeting links automatically.

Full-screen overlays

When a meeting is about to start, a full-screen overlay appears with a one-click join button. Impossible to miss.

Smart link detection

Automatically finds Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, and other meeting links in your calendar events.

Privacy-first

No telemetry, no analytics, no data sent to third parties. Your calendar data stays on your Mac.
