Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Overview

Unmissable is a macOS menu bar application that helps you join meetings on time. This policy describes how the app handles your data.

The short version: Unmissable processes your calendar data locally on your Mac. It does not send your data to any external server, does not use analytics or telemetry, and does not share your information with third parties.

Google Calendar Data

When you connect your Google account, Unmissable requests read-only access to your Google Calendar through the following OAuth 2.0 scopes:

  • calendar.readonly — Read your calendar events (titles, times, descriptions, and attendee lists) to detect upcoming meetings and extract meeting links.
  • calendar.calendarlist.readonly — List your calendars so you can choose which ones to monitor.
  • userinfo.email — Display which Google account is connected in the app interface.

How Your Data Is Used

Unmissable uses your calendar data exclusively to:

  • Display upcoming events in the menu bar.
  • Show full-screen meeting reminders before events start.
  • Parse meeting links (Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, etc.) from event descriptions for one-click join.

Data Storage

  • Calendar events are cached in a local SQLite database on your Mac to reduce API calls and enable offline access.
  • OAuth tokens (access token and refresh token) are stored in the macOS Keychain, protected by your system credentials.
  • No data is stored on any remote server.

Data Sharing

Unmissable does not share, sell, transfer, or disclose your Google user data or any other personal data to any third party. There are no exceptions to this policy.

Data Retention

Calendar data is cached locally and automatically refreshed on each sync. When you disconnect your Google account or uninstall the app, all stored data (cached events, OAuth tokens, and account information) is removed.

Apple Calendar Data

When you grant access to Apple Calendar via the macOS system permission prompt, Unmissable reads your events using the EventKit framework. This data is processed identically to Google Calendar data: locally only, with no external transmission.

Analytics and Telemetry

Unmissable does not collect analytics, crash reports, usage statistics, or any form of telemetry. The app makes no network requests other than Google Calendar API calls and OAuth token operations.

Children's Privacy

Unmissable is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, open an issue on the GitHub repository.
